Management Tools

Part of good teaching is having good systems to help the teacher manage the classroom. Tech tools to assist in management include management of participation structures and data collection. A tool we've developed include a stations tool for stations teaching, a participation structure. Another tool we've developed include a data management system to help teachers make digiital portfolios of their students' work so that they can declutter their classrooms and have certain student work ready to show parents during parent teacher confderences.
